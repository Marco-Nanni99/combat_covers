Our Story

Combat Cover was born from a problem every MMA athlete knows too well.

After spending thousands of hours training as a lifelong martial artist and UFC fighter, Jean-Paul Lebosnoyani experienced firsthand the scratches, cuts, and skin infections caused by exposed glove Velcro. Like most fighters, he relied on wrapping tape around his gloves before every session — a time-consuming fix that can leave sticky residue and wear down the gloves over time.

Jean-Paul Lebosnoyani UFC Welterweight

Knowing there had to be a better solution, the Combat Cover team set out to create one.

The Solution

After extensive prototyping and real-world testing, Combat Cover was developed: a simple, reusable sleeve that rolls over your glove's Velcro in seconds, protecting you and your training partners while helping extend the life of your equipment.

Our Mission

Built by fighters, for fighters, Combat Cover is designed to eliminate an everyday frustration so you can spend less time taping your gloves and more time focused on training. What started as a solution to one of the sport's most common problems has become a product we're proud to share with the entire MMA community.
