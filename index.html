The Problem

Designed for
Real Training

Combat Covers solve a simple but overlooked problem: exposed velcro during sparring. By creating a secure, low-profile barrier, they protect your training partners without compromising performance or glove fit.

01 — Safety

No More Velcro
Scratches

A seamless outer layer prevents exposed hook surfaces from catching skin or fabric during sparring, grappling, and clinch work.

02 — Fit

Locked-In
Stability

Silicone inner lining grips the glove surface, ensuring zero slippage during grappling, striking, or transitions — no mid-round adjustment.

03 — Comfort

Built for Long
Sessions

Breathable, moisture-wicking spandex keeps you comfortable and performing through intense rounds without distraction or heat build-up.

04 — Compatibility

One Fit.
All Gloves.

Designed to stretch across MMA, boxing, and hybrid glove systems. One size fits the wrist base of most combat gloves.
