1.Scope

  • a.How we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you visit our Site, purchase, or interact with us.

  • a.Identifiers: name, email, phone, billing/shipping address, IP, device IDs.
  • b.Commercial info: products viewed, cart, orders, returns.

  • d.Internet activity: pages viewed, referral URLs, timestamps, cookies.

  • f.Optional: marketing preferences; SMS consent.

3.How We Collect

  • b.Automatically via cookies, pixels, analytics.

  • b.Operate, secure, and improve the Site/services.
  • c.Customer support and account management.

  • f.Analytics, debugging, fraud prevention, legal compliance.

5.Cookies and Tracking

  • a.We use cookies/pixels for functionality, analytics, and ads.
  • b.Manage via browser settings and our cookie banner (if implemented).
  • c.Some features may not work without certain cookies.

6.Sharing of Information

  • a.Service providers: Shopify, payment processors, shipping/logistics, analytics (e.g., Google), marketing (e.g., Klaviyo), customer support tools.
